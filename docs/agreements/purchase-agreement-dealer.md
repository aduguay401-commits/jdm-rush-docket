## Contract Agreement – Dealer Purchase

WITNESSETH that in consideration of the mutual agreements herein contained, the parties agree as follows:

### 1. Purchase Commitment
The Client agrees to purchase the Vehicle specified, subject to its availability through a dealer in Japan.

### 2. Brokerage and Dealer Representation
JDM Rush Imports Inc. will act solely as a broker and representative in the dealer transaction, managing the importation process from Japan to Canada.

### 3. Dealer Purchase Process
JDM Rush Imports Inc. will source available vehicles through its network of Japanese dealers on behalf of the Client, using the Client's specified direction, requirements, and budget. The Client acknowledges that inventory availability can change rapidly and final selection is subject to dealer confirmation.

### 4. Vehicle Sourcing & Purchase Process
- JDM Rush Imports Inc. will issue a deposit invoice of CAD $1,500 to initiate the dealer search and sourcing process on behalf of the Client.
- JDM Rush Imports will provide the Client with a curated list of available vehicles from reputable Japanese dealers that meet the Client's criteria and budget.
- Professional sourcing consultation and advisory services will be provided throughout the process to ensure optimal selection.
- Upon receipt of the CAD $1,500 deposit, the Client may evaluate multiple vehicle options until a suitable dealer-sourced vehicle is confirmed.
- JDM Rush Imports Inc. reserves the right to pause or discontinue the sourcing process if the Client declines multiple qualified vehicle options or disregards recommendations based on dealer reliability, condition, or pricing. In such cases, CAD $1,000 of the deposit will be refunded within 15 days.
- The Client acknowledges that all associated costs—including dealer fees, shipping, marine insurance, inland transport, export agent fees, import agency fees, brokerage, taxes, and duties—will be in addition to the vehicle sale price and must be paid in full as outlined in JDM Rush Imports invoices.

### 5. Payment Handling

**(a) Deposit.** The Client shall remit a security deposit of CAD $1,500.00 to JDM Rush Imports Inc. ("JDM Rush") to initiate the sourcing process. This deposit secures the Client's commitment and compensates JDM Rush for sourcing, consultation, and coordination services.

**(b) Standard Purchases (≤ CAD $50,000).** For vehicles with a purchase price of CAD $50,000.00 or less, the Client shall remit the full remaining balance to JDM Rush upon confirmation of purchase from the Japanese dealer/export agent. JDM Rush shall then remit payment to the export agent on the Client's behalf and manage all immediate costs associated with the purchase.

**(c) High-Value Purchases (> CAD $50,000).** For vehicles with a purchase price exceeding CAD $50,000.00, JDM Rush shall facilitate the introduction of the Client to the Japanese export agent. In such cases, all payments for the purchase price of the vehicle shall be made by the Client directly to the export agent. JDM Rush shall not receive, hold, or transfer such funds.

**(d) Assumption of Risk by Client.** The Client expressly acknowledges and agrees that:
- JDM Rush is not a party to the sale contract between the Client and the export agent for vehicles exceeding CAD $50,000.00.
- JDM Rush makes no representations or warranties, express or implied, regarding the export agent, the financial transfer, or the security of the funds once transmitted.
- The Client assumes all risk and responsibility for wiring funds to the export agent and shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless JDM Rush from and against any claims, losses, disputes, chargebacks, fraud, non-performance, or other liabilities arising from or related to such payment.

**(e) Commencement of Services.** JDM Rush's obligations with respect to shipping, customs clearance, and delivery services shall commence only after written confirmation has been received from the export agent that the Client's payment has been received in full and cleared.

### 6. Payment Terms
- A CAD $1,500 deposit is required to begin sourcing vehicles.
- Full payment of the balance is due within two (2) business days of confirmed vehicle purchase.
- Payments must include any applicable bank fees or transaction costs.

### 7. Deposit and Refunds
Of the CAD $1,500 deposit, CAD $500 is non-refundable to cover administrative and sourcing-related expenses. Refunds of the remaining $1,000 are available under the terms outlined in Section 8.

### 8. Purchase Cancellation & Deposit Refund Policy
- **Cancellation Notification:** The Client may cancel their intention to purchase prior to vehicle confirmation.
- **Cancellation Process:** Written notice must be sent to JDM Rush Imports Inc. via email.
- **Refund of Deposit:** A refund of CAD $1,000 will be issued within 15 business days of cancellation confirmation. The non-refundable CAD $500 will be retained.
- **Late Cancellation:** If cancellation occurs after a vehicle has been confirmed and secured, the full deposit becomes non-refundable.

### 9. Non-Possession by Broker
JDM Rush Imports Inc. does not take physical possession of the Vehicle at any time.

### 10. Representation and Coordination
JDM Rush Imports Inc. will coordinate with all parties involved in the export from Japan and import into Canada, including customs agencies and shipping providers.

### 11. Logistics and Insurance
JDM Rush Imports Inc. will arrange for the Vehicle's shipping to the designated Canadian port using full marine insurance, via Roll-on Roll-off (RoRo) or containerized shipping, based on the best available option. The Client will be informed of the shipping schedule and vessel details.

### 12. Liability for Dealer Purchases
JDM Rush Imports Inc. is not responsible for discrepancies in vehicle condition or documentation not disclosed by the Japanese dealer. All vehicles are purchased as-is, and JDM Rush Imports Inc. does not guarantee condition beyond the information provided by the dealer or accompanying inspection reports.

### 13. Additional Transportation
If requested, JDM Rush Imports Inc. can arrange further transportation of the Vehicle to any Canadian city post-clearance, at an additional cost.

### 14. Confidentiality
Client information will be used solely for the purpose of vehicle importation and will not be disclosed except where required by law.

### 15. Registration and Compliance
The Vehicle will be imported under the Client's name, and the Client is responsible for ensuring compliance with provincial and federal registration and road safety regulations.

### 16. Indemnification
The Client agrees to indemnify and hold harmless JDM Rush Imports Inc., its employees, and agents from any liability, costs, or claims related to the vehicle purchase, importation, safety certification, or Canadian provincial registration.

### 17. Acceptance of Vehicle Condition and Liability Release

**17.1 "As-Is" Vehicle Acceptance:** The Client expressly acknowledges and agrees that the Vehicle is purchased and imported on an "as is, where is" basis without any representations or warranties, expressed or implied, made by JDM Rush Imports Inc., including, but not limited to, implied warranties of merchantability, fitness for a particular purpose, condition, or quality. The Client fully understands and accepts that the Vehicle may have visible or non-visible defects, mechanical issues, wear and tear consistent with age and usage, or discrepancies in actual condition compared to photographs, descriptions, or inspection reports provided by the Japanese dealer.

**17.2 No Responsibility for Hidden Defects:** The Client acknowledges that JDM Rush Imports Inc. is acting solely as an intermediary and broker between the Client and the dealer in Japan, and therefore has no control or authority over dealer representations, disclosures, or condition reports. JDM Rush Imports Inc. shall not be responsible or liable for hidden or latent defects, undisclosed conditions, prior accident history, odometer discrepancies, rust, corrosion, mechanical reliability, or other issues not clearly identified at the time of purchase.
