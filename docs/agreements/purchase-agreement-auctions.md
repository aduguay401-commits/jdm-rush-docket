## Contract Agreement

WITNESSETH that in consideration of the mutual agreements herein contained, the parties agree as follows:

### 1. Purchase Commitment
The Client agrees to purchase the Vehicle specified, subject to its availability at auction in Japan.

### 2. Brokerage and Auction Representation
JDM Rush Imports Inc. will act solely as a broker and representative in the auction transaction, managing the importation process from Japan to Canada.

### 3. Auction Purchase Process
JDM Rush Imports Inc. will participate in the JDM auction bidding process on behalf of the Client using the Client's specified direction, requirements, and budget. The Client acknowledges that auction purchases are subject to bidding and may not always result in a successful purchase.

### 4. Auction Bidding Process
JDM Rush Imports Inc. will issue a deposit invoice of CAD $1,500 to initiate the bidding process on behalf of the Client.

JDM Rush Imports will provide the Client with a weekly list of vehicles coming up at JDM auctions that meet the Client's specific vehicle criteria and budget. Professional auction bidding consultation and advisory services will be provided throughout the process to ensure bid success.

Upon receipt of the CAD $1,500 deposit, the Client may make as many vehicle auction bids as desired, within reason, until a successful auction bid is secured.

JDM Rush Imports Inc. reserves the right to stop the bidding process if the Client refuses to place competitive bids based on JDM auction sales history (provided by JDM Rush Imports) or disregards JDM Rush Imports Inc.'s advisory and recommendations. If JDM Rush Imports Inc. cancels the bidding process, CAD $1,000 of the deposit will be refunded within 15 days of cancellation.

The Client acknowledges that all associated costs, including auction fees, shipping, marine insurance, inland transport, export agent fees, import agency fees, brokerage, taxes, and duties, will be in addition to the JDM auction bid price and must be paid in full as outlined in JDM Rush Imports invoices.

### 5. Payment Handling
The Client shall remit a deposit payment of CAD $1,500 directly to JDM Rush Imports before bidding at auction. The full remaining balance is owed upon successful bid and purchase confirmation. JDM Rush Imports Inc. will handle payment to the auction house and cover all related expenses, including vehicle cost, auction fees, and any immediate auction house charges.

### 6. Payment Terms
- A CAD $1,500 deposit is required to initiate the bidding process.
- Full payment of the balance is due within two (2) business days of the auction purchase confirmation.
- Payments must include any applicable bank fees or transaction costs.

### 7. Deposit and Refunds
A deposit of CAD $1,500 is required to initiate the auction bidding process. Of this deposit, CAD $500 is non-refundable to cover administrative and auction entry costs incurred by JDM Rush Imports Inc.

The refundable portion (CAD $1,000) will be returned to the Client if cancellation occurs as outlined in Section 8.

### 8. Purchase Cancellation & Deposit Refund Policy
- **Cancellation Notification:** The Client may cancel their purchase intention without penalty up to 24 hours before the scheduled auction bidding time.
- **Cancellation Process:** Written notice of cancellation must be sent to JDM Rush Imports Inc. via email no less than 24 hours before the auction bid.
- **Refund of Deposit:** Timely cancellations entitle the Client to a refund of CAD $1,000. The non-refundable portion (CAD $500) will be retained to cover preparatory expenses.
- **Late Cancellation:** If cancellation occurs less than 24 hours before the scheduled auction bid, the full deposit becomes non-refundable.
- **Refund Processing:** Refunds will be processed within 15 business days of cancellation confirmation.

### 9. Non-Possession by Broker
JDM Rush Imports Inc. does not take physical possession of the Vehicle at any time.

### 10. Representation and Coordination
JDM Rush Imports Inc. will coordinate with all parties involved in the export from Japan and import into Canada, including customs agencies and shipping contacts.

### 11. Logistics and Insurance
JDM Rush Imports Inc. will arrange for the Vehicle's shipping to the designated Canadian port using full marine insurance via Roll-on Roll-off (RoRo) or containerized shipping, depending on the best available option. The Client will be informed of the shipping schedule and vessel details.

### 12. Liability for Auction Purchases
JDM Rush Imports Inc. is not responsible for discrepancies in vehicle condition or documentation not disclosed by the auction house. All auction vehicles are bought as-is, and JDM Rush Imports Inc. does not guarantee the condition or completeness of vehicles purchased at auction.

### 13. Additional Transportation
If requested by the Client, JDM Rush Imports Inc. can arrange further transportation of the Vehicle to any Canadian city at an additional cost, post-clearance.

### 14. Confidentiality
JDM Rush Imports Inc. will use the Client's information solely for the purpose of importing the Vehicle and will not disclose this information unless required by law.

### 15. Registration and Compliance
The Vehicle will be imported under the Client's name, and the Client is responsible for ensuring the vehicle complies with all provincial and federal regulations for registration and road use.

### 16. Indemnification
The Client absolves and holds harmless JDM Rush Imports Inc., its employees, and agents from any liabilities, including but not limited to, costs or issues related to the auction, Vehicle's importation, safety inspections, and registration.
